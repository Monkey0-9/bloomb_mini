## Why SatTrade?

The Bloomberg Terminal has spent 40 years building a fragmented legacy system. SatTrade has rebuilt that infrastructure from zero, layering real-time satellite intelligence on top of global market data to provide an irreplaceable edge.

### Core Features

#### 1. Institutional Exchange Strip
- **19 Global Indices Live**: S&P 500, NASDAQ, DOW, FTSE, DAX, Nikkei, Hang Seng, Sensex, and core commodities/crypto.
- **Instant Interaction**: Click any index to activate deep-dive monitoring.
- **Seamless Ticker**: Continuous horizontal scroll with HFR status integration.

#### 2. Global Equities Universe
- **100 Monitored Stocks**: High-density monitoring across NYSE, NASDAQ, LSE, TSE, HKEX, and NSE.
- **Satellite Signal Badges**: Instant visibility into orbital intelligence direction (BULLISH/BEARISH).
- **Sigma Scoring**: Quantitative signal strength bars based on proprietary overpass metrics.

#### 3. The Irreplaceable Edge: Signal-Price Overlay
- **Leading Indicator Charts**: Predictive satellite signal lines rising before price movements follow.
- **Directional Earnings**: Integrated E↑ and E↓ markers synchronized with predictive signals.
- **Overpass Telemetry**: View Sentinel-2 and SAR (Radar) overpass times directly on the timeline.

#### 4. Full Command Intelligence
- **Bloomberg-Style CLI**: Navigate via command line (AAPL, WMT, SIGNAL PORT, HELP).
- **Signal Matrix**: Sortable, sortable table of every monitored location with IC (Inf. Coeff) scoring.
- **Interactive Globe**: 3D Earth Hub with AIS vessel tracking and thermal hotspots.
